# Demo Video



This video shows our Romi robot completing the obstacle course. The robot follows the track using its line sensors, detects obstacles with the bump sensors, and uses encoder and IMU feedback to navigate turns and recover from collisions.



In this run, the robot successfully passes each checkpoint in the correct order and pushes the plastic cups out of their designated circles. This demonstration highlights the overall performance and effectiveness of our Romi.
